# VIP Lift Nigeria Design System

## Visual Theme
VIP Lift uses a Tesla-inspired direction: photography-first, minimal chrome, high whitespace, and a precise engineering tone. The interface should feel like a modern lift showroom rather than a traditional construction website.

## Color Tokens
- Electric Blue `#3E6AE1`: primary CTA and active action color.
- Carbon Dark `#171A20`: headings, footer surface, and high-emphasis text.
- Graphite `#393C41`: body copy and secondary UI text.
- Pewter `#5C5E62`: captions, metadata, and low-emphasis text.
- Light Ash `#F4F4F4`: alternate page sections and cards.
- White `#FFFFFF`: main canvas and secondary CTA surface.

## Typography
Use system sans-serif typography with restrained weights. Headings use weight 600 with tight tracking, body text uses weight 400, and navigation/buttons use weight 600. Avoid decorative display fonts.

## Components
- Primary buttons: Electric Blue fill, white text, 4px radius, compact Tesla-like proportions.
- Secondary buttons: white fill, Graphite text, 4px radius.
- Cards: minimal Light Ash surfaces, 12px radius, no heavy shadows.
- Navigation: fixed, translucent white, centered links, compact labels.
- Hero sections: full-viewport or near-full-viewport image-led layouts with one clear message and two CTAs.

## Do
- Let lift/building imagery carry the visual weight.
- Keep one message per major section.
- Preserve VIP Lift business facts exactly.
- Use static `tel:` and `mailto:` contact actions.

## Do Not
- Do not copy Tesla marks, vehicle content, or branding.
- Do not add WordPress, cart, search, CMS, or backend form behavior.
- Do not fabricate certifications, partners, or project names.
- Do not use lorem ipsum or old WordPress attribution.
